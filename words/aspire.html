aspire

/əˈspʌɪə/

late Middle English: from French aspirer or Latin aspirare, from ad- ‘to’ + spirare ‘breathe’.

  • verb
    • direct one's hopes or ambitions towards achieving something.
    • rise high; tower.
    • To have a great ambition or ultimate goal; desire strongly.
    • To strive toward an end or condition.
    • Archaic To rise high; move upwards.
    • To breathe to or into.
